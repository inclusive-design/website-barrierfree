Target Audience

The developments in this project are critical to any learner or learning context that does not fit the model of the typical student and the typical learning environment. This includes learners with disabilities, with unique learning styles, using atypical client devices, learning in unusual environments and learners approaching the topic from different perspectives or knowledge backgrounds.

The Inclusive Learning Exchange includes communities of learners who have been marginalized by conventional education systems in Canada. Among these communities are learners with disabilities, learners enrolled in adult literacy programmes, transitional programmes and academic upgrading programmes. TILE provides an opportunity to pool, share, re-purpose and collaboratively or cumulatively author accessible learning content. Through its many partners, the project addresses learners in K to 12, in post-secondary institutions, in the workplace and adult learners seeking academic upgrading.
