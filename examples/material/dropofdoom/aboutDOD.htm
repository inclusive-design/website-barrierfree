Known Use Issues:

  1. Keyboard navigation into the Flash plug-in (tabbing) only works with Internet Explorer, version 5 and higher.
  2. The number of TAB presses to enter the plug-in differs according to the browser setup.
  3. The instructions button is not read properly when focus is first switched to it. After that, the label will read but ENTER will not press the button. You must shift-TAB the focus on to the button in order for ENTER to work. All the other buttons work properly.
  4. Pressing ENTER multiple times in quick succession may cause the sounds to be played strangely.

Know Content Issues:

  1. The track height does change sufficiently to accurately represent the difference between 10m and 100m.
  2. The ball appears to travel at a constant speed down the track.
