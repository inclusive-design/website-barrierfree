Ep due to Condition

 

An object or system with potential energy (Ep) has the potential to do work. This may be due to its position or condition. You may consider it as stored energy due to some work done on the object or system.

Take the example of a spring. Initially the spring has no potential energy. A force F compresses the spring. The force F acting over a distance does work on the spring. The work done is stored in the compressed spring; the spring has potential energy due to condition. Now the compressed spring able to apply a force on another object, it has potential to do work.
