LTA & ATRC:
Partners in Barrierfree Learning

This CD ROM contains a sample of content produced by the Adaptive Technology Resource Centre (ATRC) and the Learning & Skills Television of Alberta Limited (LTA) for the Barrier – Free and TILE projects. For a more extensive explanation of these projects, please see the written materials accompanying this portfolio. To accompany the video based content utilized by the Barrier Free Project, various electronic learning objects were also developed. Students using the Barrier- Free Video player were able to watch the video, and link to these learning objects at appropriate times from within the video itself.

The Barrier –Free Player, Authoring Tool, video content, and all learning objects developed were designed to be accessible to people with disabilities. Samples of these learning objects follow. The first link included is to the learning object itself, while the second link connects to the meta-data developed for each learning object. The accompanying meta-data has been produced by Turbo XML according to the standards and conventions of CanCore. Once TILE is completed, these learning objects will be made available to educators through the TILE learning objects repository.
