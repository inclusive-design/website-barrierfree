Speed from Mech. Energy

 

Provided all other variables are known, the mechanical energy approach, used in the gymnast example, can be used to determine the speed of an object or system at its lowest point.

Ek(bottom) = Ep(top)
½ (mv2) = mgh

divide both sides by m:

½ (mv2) = mgh
½ (v2) = gh
v2 = 2gh
v = square root of (2gh)
