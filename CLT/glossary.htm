Glossary

 

displacement
a change in position, distance, with direction
energy
the ability to do work; energy exists in a variety of forms: thermal, kinetic, chemical potential, etc.
kinetic energy
the energy an object or system has due to its motion
mechanical energy
the sum of kinetic and potential energy of an object
potential energy
the energy of an object or system due to its position or condition, e.g., gravitational potential energy, chemical potential energy
scalar
a quantity which has only a magnitude
thermal energy
the total energy of all the particles of a substance; changes in thermal energy are measured as changes in temperature
vector
a quantity which has both magnitude and direction
work
the transfer of energy from one object or system to another when a force is applied over a distance
