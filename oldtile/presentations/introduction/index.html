Educational Breakdown

  • Mismatch of pace
  • Mismatch of knowledge assumptions
  • Mismatch of learning approach with teaching approach
  • Mismatch of motivators and feedback
  • Mismatch of sequence or path
  • Access barriers

Outcomes of E-learning

  • Does not save money
  • Does not save instructor time
  • Does not shift the Bell curve higher
  • Does compress the Bell curve
  • Implications for marginalized students
  • Greater opportunity for personalization

Beyond No Significant Difference

Opportunity to:

  • Emulate one-on-one teaching
  • Address educational breakdown

Learner-Centric Education

  • Not topic filtering
  • Personalize:
    • Pace and path of learning
    • Presentation of learning material
    • Control of learning environment
    • Learning approach
    • Selective content filtering
