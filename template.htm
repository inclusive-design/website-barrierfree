The Barrierfree Learning Environment

The Barrierfree Project aims to make Internet multimedia learning materials that are rich in audio and video accessible to people with disabilities. Sound can be used to explain a video to people who are blind. Text and graphics can be used to replace sound for people who are deaf. Flexible playback controls can allow people with physical disabilities to navigate in a way that suits them. Everyone benefits from the resulting new ways to experience the material.
