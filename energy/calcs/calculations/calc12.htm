12. Air Table Example: Calculating Energy from Timed Displacement (22:28, 23:04, 23:25, 23:45, 24:26, 24:40, 25:20, 25:41, 26:50)

d = 0.0167 m
t = 0.050 s
v = d ÷ t

v = 0.334 m/s

Calculating Maximum Kinteric Energy:

(Ek)max = ½ × m × v2
(Ek)max = ½ × 0.558 kg × (0.334 m/s)2
(Ek)max = 3.1 × 10-2 J

Calculating Gravitational Potential Energy:

hpuck ÷ lpuck = htable ÷ ltable
hpuck = (htable ÷ ltable) × lpuck
hpuck = (htable × lpuck) ÷ ltable

hpuck = 0.098 m ÷ 0.652 m × 0.039 m
hpuck = 0.00586 m

(Ep)max = m × g × h
(Ep)max = 0.558 kg × 9.81 m/s2 × 0.00586 m
(Ep)max = 3.2 × 10-2 J
