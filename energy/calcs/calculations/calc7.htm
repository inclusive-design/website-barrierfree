7. Rope Race Example: Calculating Work and Power (7:34, 7:44, 8:00, 8:22)

Length of rope: 8.0 m
Height climbed: 5.75 m
Mass of left contestant: 85 kg
Mass of right contestant: 85 kg

Contestant on the left:

Setting Up:
m = 85 kg
g = 9.81 m/s2
h = 5.75 m
W = F × d
W = Fg × h
W = m × g × h

Calculating Work:
W = m × g × h
W = 85 kg × 9.81 m/s2 × 5.75 m
W = 4795 J
W = 4.8 × 103 J

Calculating Power:

W = 4795 J
t = 9.0 s
P = W ÷ t
P = 4795 J ÷ 9.0 s
P = 533 J/s
